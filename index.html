About

Story Is
The Strategy

Double Strings is a film and television production company based in Los Angeles and New York, developing original projects with unflinching ambition and uncompromising craft.

We specialize in character-driven storytelling and IP-driven development — building original intellectual property from the ground up and adapting compelling real-world stories into premium drama, thriller, and comedy.

From FBI procedurals rooted in true events to original franchise-ready IP, every project we develop is built to last. We believe the most powerful stories are the ones that are both commercially viable and genuinely necessary.
